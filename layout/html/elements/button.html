Elements of tip button are there so you can push them, in order to triger an action.

They don't have labels.

Their value is their text.

Forms will ignore buttons.

E1 - Press me, and nothing will happen!

E2 - Press me, to do something!

  • The buttons may be pressed only as long as they are not readonly.

E3 - Icon buttons

  • Buttons may have icons.
  • The icons may be displayed to the left or to the right of the text

Ex. 4 - Buttons displayed inside other ACE fields.

  • Up to 2 buttons may be displayed within other fields, with the help of 2 helper classes.
  • How to do this:
    • Create a div to contain the target ACE element, and the additional button field, or fields.
    • Create the ACE field: textbox, combobox, autocomplete, etc.
    • Create the button field immidietly after.
    • Give the button field the class ace-inside.
    • If a second button is present, give the second button both this classes: ace-inside and ace-inside-double.
  • In this example, we have placed one or two buttons in a few fields of different types.
