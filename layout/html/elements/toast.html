Elements of tip toast are used to display simple notifications which do not interfere with the user's activity.

There are 4 types of toasts, each with their own color and icon:

  • success
  • warning
  • error
  • help

All toast windows will close themselves after a few seconds. Toasts of type help will close themselves after 10 seconds, and all other after 2 seconds. You can forcely close them by clicking on them.
