One of the types of panels predefined in the ace classes.

When displayed, the independed forms cover all available space, going over all existing content.

Ex. 1 - Independent Form

  • To define an independed form, use the class: ace-independent-form.
  • To show it, use the ace-show class.
  • To hide it, remove the ace-show class.
  • To alter the direction from which it is shown, use one of the following classes:
    • ace-lateral-form: when applied to an independent form, it will force it to only cover a small horizontal section on the side of the screen.
