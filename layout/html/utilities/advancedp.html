One of the types of panels predefined in the ace classes.

It is basically a vertical section of the screen, which is designed to slide over the existing content of the page, from left, right, top, or bottom.

Ex. 1 - AdvancedP

  • To define an independed form, use the class: ace-advanced-panel.
  • To show it, use the ace-show class.
  • To hide it, remove the ace-show class.
  • To alter the direction from which it is shown, use one of the following classes:
    • from-top
    • from-right
    • from-bottom
    • from-left
