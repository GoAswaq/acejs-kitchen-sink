Title Bars are regions which are designed to act as placeholders for various title elements: texts, buttons, etc.

ACE has two classes designed to create a title region: ace-title-bar and ace-transparent-title-bar.

Ex. 1 - Title Bar

  • To define a title bar, use the class: ace-title-bar.
  • The class ace-buttons-groups may be used for the container that holds together the buttons in the title bar.

Ex. 2 - Transparent Title Bar

  • To define a title bar, use the class: ace-transparent-title-bar.
  • The class ace-buttons-groups may be used for the container that holds together the buttons in the title bar.
